Join the Jackson Symphony Youth Orchestra

Serious young musicians who play any string instrument, standard orchestral brass and woodwinds, or percussion are invited to audition for The Jackson Youth Symphony.

Auditions are held annually in the fall at Union University, but under certain circumstances, students can be accepted later in the semester or at the beginning of the second semester in the spring.


2016 Audition Dates

August 13th 10am-3pm

August 14th 2pm-5pm

Audition times will be assigned to you after your audition form and fee have been turned into The Jackson Symphony Office.
